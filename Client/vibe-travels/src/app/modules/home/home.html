About VibeTravels

VibeTravels helps you organize fragmented travel ideas and convert them into coherent, actionable plans. Whether you're planning a week-long adventure or exploring activity options for a destination, our AI-powered system creates personalized itineraries based on your preferences.

Simply capture your travel thoughts in notes, set your preferences for travel style and accommodations, and let VibeTravels generate detailed plans tailored to your trip length and group size.
