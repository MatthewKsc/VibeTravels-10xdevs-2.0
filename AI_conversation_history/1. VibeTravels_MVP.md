# App – VibeTravels (MVP)

### Main problem
Planning engaging and interesting trips is hard. By leveraging AI’s potential, creativity, and knowledge, VibeTravels lets you turn simplified notes about places and travel goals into concrete plans.

### Minimum feature set
- Saving, reading, browsing, and deleting notes for upcoming trips
- A simple user account system to associate users with their notes
- A user profile page for storing travel preferences
- AI integration that converts notes into detailed plans, taking into account preferences, time, number of people, and potential places and attractions

### Out of scope for the MVP
- Sharing trip plans between accounts
- Rich multimedia handling and analysis (e.g., photos of places to visit)
- Advanced time and logistics planning

### Success criteria
- 90% of users have their travel preferences filled out in their profile
- 75% of users generate 3 or more trip plans per year
